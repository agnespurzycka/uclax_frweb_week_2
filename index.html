Listen

Listen is the fourth album by the British rock band by The Kooks

It was released in September 2014 through Universal

The album differs from the band's previous work as elements of Jazz, Gospel and R&B were added into the music

It was recorded in Los Angeles and London

Upon it's release Listen debuted no.16 on the UK Albums Chart
